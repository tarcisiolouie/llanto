Introduction

COVID-19 Pandemic is not the first-ever pandemic which hit this glob, but it is the deadliest and possesses enough capability to shake the so-called developed global system. In the present scenario other than the virus livelihood becomes a prime issue to be discussed. Especially for the poor community who are dependent on farming and labour work. This paper has critically discussed about Livelihoods and its possible restoration during the COVID-19 Pandemic time.
